## 2. Description of Service

OpenOcto is a self-hosted, open-source personal AI assistant constructor with voice control and a persona system. The Service is designed to run locally on user-owned hardware and provides:

- Voice-controlled interaction with AI language models
- A web-based administration panel for configuration and monitoring
- Integration with third-party AI backends (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Ollama, and other providers)
- Text-to-speech and speech-to-text capabilities
- Configurable AI personas with customizable behavior

The Service acts as an **intermediary layer** between the user and third-party AI models. OpenOcto does not independently generate AI responses — all AI-generated content originates from the configured third-party AI backends.
